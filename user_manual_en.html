DHL Express Shipment Creation - Backup Solution (Test Environment)

User Manual

Version: 2.0 (Revised)
Date: 2025-12-17

1. Introduction

This document provides a step-by-step guide for using the DHL Express Shipment Creation (Backup Solution). This tool ensures business continuity by allowing you to manually create shipments, generate Air Waybills (AWB), and manage invoices during system maintenance windows.

URL: https://sbs-e2e.vercel.app/

2. Getting Started

  1. Access the Site: Open the URL in your browser.
  2. Consent: Check the "I agree to the Terms of Use" box.
  3. Start: Click "Create Shipment" button.

3. Shipment Workflow (7 Steps)

Step 1: Address

  • Shipper (Origin): Verify your Name, Company, Phone, and Address.
  • Receiver (Destination):
    • Select Country first to load specific validation rules for that destination.
    • Enter Postal Code and City.
    • Fill in Contact Details (Name, Company, Phone, Email).

Step 2: Shipment Details

  • Shipment Date: Choose the date you plan to ship.
  • Type: Select Document or Package.
  • Invoice Options (For Packages):
    • Create Invoice: The system generates a digital invoice for you.
    • Use My Own: You will upload your own invoice file in Step 5.

Step 3: Package Details

  • Line Items: List the contents (Description, Qty, Value, Weight).
  • Add Piece: Specify the Weight (kg) & Dimensions (cm) for each box.
  • Note: Ensure all items are declared correctly for customs.

Step 4: Payment

  • Shipper Account: Enter your 9-digit DHL Account Number.
  • Payment Terms:
    • Shipper Pays: You pay.
    • Receiver Pays: You must provide the Receiver's valid Account Number.
  • Incoterm: Select the term (e.g., DAP, DDP, EXW).

Step 5: Documents

  • Uploads:
    • If you chose Use My Own Invoice, you must upload it here.
    • You can also upload other necessary documents (permits, licenses).

Step 6: Pickup

  • Schedule Pickup?
    • Yes: Select Date, Time Window, Location (e.g. Reception), and Instructions.
    • No: Choose this if you will drop off the package at a DHL Service Point yourself.

Step 7: Summary & Confirm

  • Review all details in the summary card.
  • Print Size: Select your preferred format:
    • A4: For standard office printers.
    • Label: For 4x6 thermal label printers.
  • Click "Create Shipment" to finalize.

4. Confirmation

Upon success, you will see the confirmation screen:

  • AWB Number: Your tracking number.
  • Download:
    • Label: The shipping label (Required).
    • Invoice: Customs invoice (Print 2 copies).
    • Receipt: Proof of shipment.
